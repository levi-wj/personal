About page

Introduction

My name is Levi Jones. I am a front and back-end developer currently going to school in Rexburg, ID. I love solving problems in creative ways and am always looking to learn new things.

Skills

Front-end: I have a strong background in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I have worked with jQuery and Bootstrap
Back-end: I have experience with MySQL, C, Python, C#, and have used Firebase and AWS
Testing: I have written many automated unit tests using Selenium and Python
Games: I have worked with Unity and Unreal Engine, building to PC and Android, and have used Blender to make 3D models and rig for animation
I am fluent with the git and GitHub workflows, and am very comfortable with the Linux command line.

Goals

I am looking to get a job doing programming where I can stretch myself and learn new things every day.
I would love to work on a team where I can work on a variety of projects.
I am passionate about front-end design and game development.
